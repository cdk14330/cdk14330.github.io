March Madness

By Carl Kaufman

College basketball is one of the most popular things for sports fans to watch. As the season comes to an end, every team is looking for a spot in the NCAA tournament. It is so popular to watch because it is usually known for its upsets and cindrella stories. These are some of the most notable teams to make it all the way to the Final Four as huge underdogs:

  • George Mason University, 2006
  • Virginia Commonwealth University, 2011
  • University of South Carolina, 2017

In 2016, the final game of the tournament, The NCAA National Championship, was the most exciting game. It was a hard fought battle between Villanova and North Carolina which went back and forth but went to Villanova after a buzzer beating 3 point shot. Here is the box score of the game:

This game between these two evenly matched teams came down to the wire. North Carolina over came a deficit in the final minutes of the game and tied it up with 4.7 seconds remaining. Then Villanova player, Kris Jenkins, hit the game winning three as the buzzer sounded.
